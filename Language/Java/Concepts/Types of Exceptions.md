### **1️⃣ Checked Exceptions**
- Checked at **compile time**
- Must be handled or declared

Examples:
- `IOException`
- `SQLException`
- `ClassNotFoundException`

---
### **2️⃣ Unchecked Exceptions**
- Occur at **runtime**
- Subclasses of `RuntimeException`

Examples:
- `NullPointerException`
- `ArithmeticException`
- `NumberFormatException`
